Iniya piranthanaal vaazhthukkal en uyire, en ulagame 😘
Romba romba naal aachu unna nerla paathu tight-aa hug panni, 
en idhayathula olinjikka. Eppo nama onna irukka porom nu, 
oru oru naalum naan manasukkulla count pannitu irukken da.
Indha year inga varala nu theriyum, but idhu ellam temporary dhaan chellam. 
Oru naal kandippa nama veetla ellaarum sandhoshama okay solla vechu, 
kai pidichu kalyaanam panni, nimmadhiyaa onna vaazhuvom. 
Andha azhagana naalukkaaga dhaan naan uyiroda wait pannitu irukken 💍

Nama seekiram andha day-a find pannanum da. 
En life-la enakku venum nu oru aasai mattum dhaan nee, 
Eppovum un kooda dhaan irukkanum, engayum pogama. Un madiyila thalai vechu, 
en ellaa kashtathayum marandhu, priyaamaa vaazhanum.

Ippovum kannukulla odudhu nama memories ellam – trips, sandai, samadhanam… 
idhellaam enakku romba precious da. Idhe ellathayum naan un kooda namma veetla, 
husband-wife aa daily share pannanum nu aasai.

Indha long distance romba valikkudhu da chellam. 
Office mudinju veetukku varum podhu nee pakkathula irundha, 
oru hugla ellaa tiredum pogum. Un kitta ellathayum solli polamba, 
nee kekkura andha neram dhaan enakku sorgam 🫂

nee enod life erukthuku naan romba lucky nu thonudhu da. Life long sandai pottu, 
appo appo kovama pesi, adichittu, thirumba love panni… en kooda dhaan iruppiya da chellam? ❤️

Enna nadandhaalum, eppovum un pakkathula dhaan naan iruppen. Enakku nee venum, 
unakku naan venum indha jenmam mattum illa, ellaa jenmathulayum.
Iing oru secret olinjirukku nu enakku theriyum… adhu enna kandupidi chlm 💖
